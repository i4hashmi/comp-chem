Introduction to Computational Chemistry

This course in computational chemistry provides a clear and accessible introduction to the fundamental concepts and practical techniques used in modern molecular modeling. Starting with the basics, it guides learners through essential topics such as quantum chemical foundations, the role and selection of basis sets, and an introduction to Density Functional Theory (DFT). Designed for students, researchers, and professionals, the course blends theory with real-world applications, enabling participants to understand, set up, and interpret computational chemistry calculations with confidence.
